Vector Gains — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

Overview

Vector Gains ("the App") is a personal fitness tracking application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data you enter into Vector Gains — including workouts, meals, body weight, caffeine intake, planner events, and app settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your iPhone using the Capacitor Preferences API. This data persists on your device until you delete the app or clear the app's data. We do not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

Vector Gains does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not contain ads.

Google Fonts

The App loads custom fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) to display themed typography. Google may collect basic connection data (such as your IP address) as part of serving these fonts. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

Vector Gains is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
